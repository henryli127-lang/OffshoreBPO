China sales and service team‑as‑a‑service for export manufacturers and hardware companies

Test and grow your China market with a local sales and service team – before you commit to a legal entity.

1

Who this is for

This solution is for industrial equipment manufacturers, hardware companies, and consumer brands exporting to China or planning to enter the market.

Typical profiles include:

  • European or North American manufacturers working with Chinese distributors or OEMs.
  • Companies with early China sales but no local staff on the ground.
  • Businesses exploring China as a strategic market but not yet ready to establish a subsidiary.
2

Your key challenges

  • You need local sales, channel, and after‑sales presence to support distributors and customers, but do not want to immediately invest in a China entity, office, and full internal team.
  • You are worried about compliance, contracts, and HR regulations if you hire people directly in China.
  • You lack an internal China team to drive local pipeline, manage relationships, and handle after‑sales service.
3

Our solution

We provide a "China team‑as‑a‑service" model that combines outsourced sales execution with compliant employment.

Local sales and channel team

We recruit and manage local sales representatives, channel managers, and business development staff who focus on building your pipeline and supporting partners and end users.

After‑sales and service capacity

Where required, we add technical service engineers or customer success staff to handle installation coordination, training, and post‑sales support.

EOR‑based employment

All staff are employed through our EOR/PEO structure in China, ensuring fully compliant contracts, payroll, and social insurance while you direct their activities and targets.

4

What you get

  • A practical way to test and grow the China market with a local team, before committing to a legal entity.
  • Clear monthly cost structure and transparent reporting on sales activity and results.
  • Ability to later transition the team into your own China entity if and when you set one up.
