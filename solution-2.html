How a China sales team‑as‑a‑service works

1

We hire and employ your local team

We recruit sales, channel, and service staff in China and employ them through our EOR/PEO solution, so you don't need a local entity.

2

You own the strategy; we run local execution

You define ideal customer profiles, pricing, and messaging; we localise and execute sales activity, channel development, and after‑sales follow‑up.

3

Compliance, HR, and operations are handled by us

We manage contracts, payroll, social insurance, and HR administration, ensuring your people are fully compliant in China.

4

Transparent reporting and scalability

We provide regular activity and pipeline reporting; as you see traction, we can expand headcount or help you transition to your own entity.

Typical go‑to‑market scenarios we support

European industrial manufacturer

Uses a small China sales and service team to win first key accounts and understand pricing and channel dynamics before committing to a subsidiary.

Hardware or IoT company

Builds a regional sales + technical pre‑sales team in East and South China to support distributors and key customers.

Specialised equipment or component supplier

Maintains a lean China presence to nurture relationships with OEMs and strategic partners.

In each case, you get the benefits of a local presence in China without the fixed cost and complexity of a local entity.
