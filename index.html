Services

What we do

We combine business process outsourcing, compliant employment solutions, and local sales execution to give you a complete China team‑as‑a‑service.

BPO: your China delivery team

We build and manage dedicated teams in China for customer support, technical support, software development, QA, data processing, and back‑office operations. You define the outcomes; we hire, manage, and optimize the team to deliver them.

EOR / PEO: compliant employment in China

We act as the legal employer for your team in China, handling employment contracts, payroll, social insurance, benefits, and day‑to‑day HR, while you direct the work. You get the talent you need without the cost, delay, and risk of setting up a local entity.

Sales and market execution in China

We assemble and run local sales, channel, and customer success teams to help you acquire and support customers in China. You bring the product and strategy; we provide the people, local know‑how, and ongoing management.
