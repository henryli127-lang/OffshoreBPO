EOR: A TURNKEY LEGAL EMPLOYER

THE EOR PROVIDER BECOMES THE LEGAL EMPLOYER OF STAFF, HANDLING ALL HR, PAYROLL, & COMPLIANCE OBLIGATIONS. HIRE WITHOUT A CHINESE ENTITY

PEO: A CO-EMPLOYMENT PARTNERSHIP

A PEO SHARES HR DUTIES WITH YOUR EXISTING CHINESE ENTITY, WHICH REMAINS THE LEGAL EMPLOYER OF RECORD

EOR vs. PEO: A STRATEGIC COMPARISON

EOR in China

  • LEGAL EMPLOYER: EOR provider is the legal employer of staff
  • LOCAL ENTITY: NOT REQUIRED: HIRE WITHOUT CHINESE COMPANY
  • COMPLIANCE RESPONSIBILITY: EOR BEARS DAY-TO-DAY COMPLIANCE & HR OBLIGATIONS
  • SPEED TO HIRE: OFTEN 2-4 WEEKS TO ONBOARD
  • TYPICAL USE CASE: MARKET ENTRY, PILOT TEAMS, DISTRIBUTED TEAMS, RISK-SENSITIVE PROJECTS
LEGAL & COMPLIANCE RISK ALLOCATION
EOR CENTRALIZES RISK WITH PROVIDER, PEO LIABILITY LARGELY REMAINS WITH YOUR ENTITY
TOTAL COST OF OWNERSHIP (TCO)
EOR LOWER FOR SMALL TEAMS (<20 FTE), AVOIDS ENTITY SETUP COSTS. PEO MORE ECONOMICAL FOR LARGER, STABLE OPERATIONS
TALENT ACCESS & GEOGRAPHIC FLEXIBILITY
EOR OFFERS MULTI-CITY COVERAGE. PEO CONSTRAINED BY ENTITY REGISTRATION LOCATION

PEO in China

  • LEGAL EMPLOYER: YOUR CHINESE ENTITY IS THE LEGAL EMPLOYER
  • LOCAL ENTITY: REQUIRED: PEO WORKS ONLY ONCE AN ENTITY EXISTS
  • COMPLIANCE RESPONSIBILITY: SHARED: LEGAL LIABILITY LARGELY REMAINS WITH YOU
  • SPEED TO HIRE: DEPENDENT ON YOUR ENTITY SETUP & INTERNAL APPROVALS
  • TYPICAL USE CASE: GROWTH PHASE, LARGER STABLE TEAMS, LONG-TERM PRESENCE COMMITMENT

WHEN EOR IS THE BETTER FIT

IDEAL FOR VALIDATING BUSINESS CASE (SMALL TEAM), AVOIDING ENTITY SETUP, REGULATED INDUSTRIES, MANAGING DYNAMIC HEADCOUNT

WHEN PEO BECOMES COMPELLING

BEST WITH A LOCAL ENTITY FOR LONG-TERM OPERATIONS, WANTING FULL CONTROL OVER EMPLOYMENT BRANDING, BUILDING LARGE, STABLE TEAMS

STEP 1: VALIDATE WITH EOR

BEGIN WITH EOR TO TEST MARKET. BUILD INITIAL TEAM, ACCELERATE TIME-TO-PRODUCTIVITY WITH MINIMAL RISK & SUNK COSTS

STEP 2: SCALE WITH A LOCAL ENTITY + PEO

Once your business case is proven and long-term commitment is justified, establish a local entity and engage a PEO to professionalize HR operations.

COMPLEMENTARY TOOLS, NOT COMPETITORS

THE MOST EFFECTIVE STRATEGY OFTEN INVOLVES STARTING WITH EOR AND TRANSITIONING TO PEO, USING THE RIGHT TOOL FOR EACH GROWTH STAGE

SELECTING A PARTNER FOR SUCCESS

  • LOOK FOR DYNAMIC COMPLIANCE MONITORING: SYSTEMS TO TRACK RECENT REGULATORY CHANGES AT MUNICIPAL LEVEL, AUTOMATICALLY ADJUST PAYROLL & SOCIAL INSURANCE
  • DEMAND TRANSPARENT, AUDIT-READY REPORTING: CONSOLIDATED REPORTS (IFRS/US GAAP COMPLIANT) FOR GLOBAL FINANCE INTEGRATION
  • ENSURE DATA SOVEREIGNTY & PRIVACY: COMPLIANCE WITH CHINA'S PIPL WHILE SUPPORTING NECESSARY CROSS-BORDER DATA FLOWS FOR GLOBAL HR PLATFORMS
