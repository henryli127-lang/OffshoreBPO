China BPO & EOR for global tech and professional services

Build high‑performing support, engineering, and operations teams in China – without setting up a local entity.

1

Who this is for

This solution is designed for SaaS, AI, IT services, digital agencies, and other knowledge‑driven companies that want to build teams in China for customer support, technical support, engineering, design, or data work.

Typical profiles include:

  • 10–500 person tech or services companies expanding into Asia‑Pacific.
  • Companies needing 24/7 coverage, faster response times, or lower unit cost.
  • Teams already comfortable with remote and distributed work models.
2

Your key challenges

  • You need to scale customer support or technical support in Asia, but do not want to wait months to set up a China entity.
  • You want to build a China engineering, QA, or data team, but are unsure how to hire and stay compliant with local labor and tax rules.
  • You lack on‑the‑ground HR and management capacity in China to recruit, onboard, and retain the right people.
3

Our solution

We provide an integrated BPO + EOR model tailored to tech and professional services companies.

Dedicated China delivery teams

We build and manage dedicated teams in China for customer support, technical support, software development, QA, data labeling, data processing, and back‑office operations.

Employer of Record (EOR) and PEO

We act as the legal employer in China, handling compliant employment contracts, payroll, social insurance, benefits, and HR administration while you manage day‑to‑day work and performance.

Tech‑savvy management and tools

We use modern collaboration and HR tools to integrate your China team with your global workflows, giving you visibility into headcount, costs, and performance.

4

What you get

  • A fully managed China team aligned to your SLAs and KPIs.
  • Legal, compliant employment without setting up a China entity.
  • Faster hiring cycles and reduced per‑head cost versus local hiring in Western markets.
