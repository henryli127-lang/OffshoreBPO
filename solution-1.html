Why China for BPO – and what can go wrong if you do it alone

China's BPO market has grown into a large, diversified ecosystem covering IT support, software development, data work, finance shared services, and customer operations. For global tech and professional services companies, China offers a deep talent pool, cost advantages, and time zone coverage for Asia‑Pacific clients.

However, building a small delivery team in China on your own can be slow and risky. You must navigate entity setup, labour law, social insurance, local HR practice, and management overhead – all before the first hire starts delivering.

Our BPO model lets you treat China as an extension of your delivery organisation: we handle hiring, HR, and operations on the ground, while you stay focused on product, clients, and KPIs.
